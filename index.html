About Rev. ICN Olang

Rev. ICN Olang is a devoted servant of God called to preach, teach, and restore lives through the Gospel of Jesus Christ. With over 15 years of ministry experience, Rev. Olang has impacted countless individuals across nations through powerful teachings, healing services, and compassionate outreach. His ministry focuses on the transformative power of God's Word to bring hope, healing, and purpose to every life.
