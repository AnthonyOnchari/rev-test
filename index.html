Our Mission

Equipping believers for successful ministry and life mission

Biblical Understanding

Equip the believer with proper understanding of God, the Church, the world, the devil and who they are in Christ to succeed in their calling.

Training & Activation

Train believers for the harvest field and activate the gifts and talents of the Holy Spirit constrained by ignorance.

Release & Restoration

Release mature believers as co-laborers with Christ and restore foundational apostolic doctrine while mending cracks in Christian leadership.

Great Commission

Facilitate aggressive propagation of the great commission through teaching the whole counsel of God without adding or subtracting.

Our Purpose

PURPOSE STATEMENT

Win people to Jesus, incorporate them into God's family, develop them to Christlike maturity, equip them for ministry and life mission in the world to glorify God.
